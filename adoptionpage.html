Virtual Adoption: Transform Lives from Afar

Adopting an animal virtually isn't just kindness; it's a lifeline for those who can't speak for themselves.

Imagine giving a neglected horse a warm bed, a hungry cat nutritious meals, or a wounded bird critical medical care—all from the comfort of your home. Virtual adoption allows you to make this difference.

What is Virtual Adoption?

Virtual adoption enables you to financially support an animal under our care without taking it home. With flexible options—monthly, quarterly, or yearly—you can help provide food, medical care, and shelter to animals in need.

Why Choose Virtual Adoption?

  • Direct Impact: 100% of your donation supports food, shelter, and medical care.
  • Tax Benefits: Your donations are eligible for deductions under 80G.
  • Personal Connection: Receive regular updates, photos, and stories about your adopted animal.

How Does Your Support Help?

At PFA Uttarakhand, we care for a diverse range of animals: horses, mules, donkeys, cats, poultry, and goats. Each requires specialized care, and your contributions help us:

  • Provide nutritious food and clean water
  • Offer lifesaving medical treatments
  • Maintain safe and comfortable shelter
  • Engage animals in enriching activities

Visit Your Adopted Animal

Want to see your impact? Visit our shelter and meet the animal you've supported. Witness the life-changing difference your kindness has made. Contact us to schedule a visit.

What If My Adopted Animal is Rehomed?

If your adopted animal finds a permanent home or no longer requires support, we’ll keep you informed and help you transfer your generosity to another animal in need.
