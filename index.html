About Us

Founded in 2012, People for Animals Uttarakhand is dedicated to creating compassionate and sustainable solutions for animal welfare in Uttarakhand and beyond. We stand at the forefront of animal care with our state-of-the-art no-tie sanctuary â€“ a pioneering approach that redefines compassion and rehabilitation. Beyond our sanctuary, we strategically work to create systemic change by developing evidence-based policies, training the next generation of animal welfare leaders, and collaborating with government agencies to ensure animals are treated with respect and dignity.

Join our mission to transform animal welfare. Whether you want to support our rescue efforts, volunteer at our sanctuary, contribute to our policy work, or spread awareness, there's a meaningful way for you to make a difference. Together, we can build a world where animals live safely, free from harm and suffering.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform the landscape of animal welfare by addressing the root causes of animal suffering. Through comprehensive sanctuary care, collaborative policy work with the government, training of animal welfare professionals, we are building a world where every animal is recognised as a living being deserving of dignity, safety, and respect.

Our Vision

We aspire to create a future where animals are not just protected, but truly valued. A future where compassion drives policy, where every animal has a chance to live free from suffering, and where human-animal coexistence is built on mutual understanding and care.

Discover Happy Home Sanctuary: A Haven for Rescued Animals in Uttarakhand

Located in the peaceful village of Chharba in Dehradun District, Happy Home Sanctuary is a unique No-Tie, No-Cage Sanctuary operated by People for Animals Uttarakhand. Our sanctuary provides a safe, stress-free environment for animals rescued from cruelty, including felines, equines, poultry birds, and goats. As Uttarakhandâ€™s only sanctuary focused on feline and equine rescue, we are committed to giving these animals compassionate care, housing, and treatment. Supported by grants from organizations like Oil and Natural Gas Corporation and India Infrastructure Finance Company Limited, we work tirelessly to ensure our residents thrive in a dignified, cruelty-free environment.

Over the course of eight months, fellows undergo rigorous training to develop the knowledge and tools necessary for impactful policy development, advocacy, and hands-on reforms in animal welfare.

Empowering Stakeholders for Better Animal Welfare

At People for Animals Uttarakhand, we recognize the crucial role that government departments play in animal welfare. Through our targeted capacity-building programs, we empower judicial officers, law enforcement agencies, and key departments such as the Animal Husbandry Department, Government Veterinary Departments, Pollution Control Boards, and Public Prosecutors. These programs focus on strengthening knowledge of animal welfare laws, ensuring that stakeholders are equipped to handle animal-related situations effectively and with compassion.

By building the capacity of these key players, we aim to create a more responsive, informed, and compassionate approach to animal welfare across Uttarakhand. Visit Website.

Strengthening Animal Welfare Through Government Partnerships

People for Animals Uttarakhand collaborates with key organizations like the Society for Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (SPCA) in Rudraprayag and Jammu, as well as the Municipal Corporations of Dehradun and Haldwani. These partnerships are instrumental in providing safe housing for rescued animals. We also manage the infirmary at SPCA Rudraprayag, where we focus on rescuing and rehabilitating abused equines, particularly those seized from the Kedarnath Yatra track.

Through these collaborations, we aim to create a broader, more effective impact on animal welfare across various regions in Uttarakhand.

Encouraging Ethical and Responsible Sourcing

At People for Animals Uttarakhand, we believe that corporations have the power to transform the future of animals through responsible sourcing. We work closely with corporates to promote ethical supply chains that prioritize animal welfare and ensure businesses operate in harmony with nature. By supporting sustainable choices, we help companies create a positive impact on both the environment and animal welfare.

Join us in making ethical sourcing a key part of your business, for a more compassionate future.

Transforming Animal Product Production: Empowering Producers for Ethical Practices

At People for Animals Uttarakhand, we recognize that producers of animal products play a vital role in ensuring animal welfare. We work closely with producers and government agencies to provide essential education, sensitization, and skill development, enabling them to adopt traditional rearing practices that can be scaled for commercial production without compromising the well-being of animals.

Our goal is to empower farmers and producers to make ethical, sustainable choices that ensure animal welfare and contribute to responsible production. By fostering these practices, we aim to create a future where animal products are produced with compassion and care.

Supporting Communities in Times of Crisis: Together for Animal Welfare

At People for Animals Uttarakhand, we are committed to supporting animal welfare organizations and individuals who step up during critical times, such as floods or heat waves. We provide essential food and medical aid to those on the front lines, ensuring that animals in distress receive the care they need during emergencies.

Our efforts aim to strengthen the collective response to animal welfare crises, making a difference when it matters most.
