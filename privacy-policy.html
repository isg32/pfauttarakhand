Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of People for Animals Uttarakhand. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you donate or fill in the message box:

  • Your name and contact details (email, phone number).
  • Information provided during donations or inquiries.
  • Usage data, such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand user behavior and improve website functionality. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To process donations and issue receipts.
  • To communicate updates and information about our activities.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We implement measures to protect your data, but please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
